How to make a ham sandwich

  1. Gather meat, cheese, ham, lettuce, mustard and mayo
  2. Lay out 2 slice of bread side by side
  3. Spread mayo and mustard onto bread, one condiment per slice
  4. Put 4-5 slices of ham on one slice of bread
  5. Top the same slice with cheese
  6. Put on the second slice of bread, condiment side down
  7. Bon Apetit!
